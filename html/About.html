    ABOUT US

    Game Bit is the leading retail chain specializing in high-end computer hardware aimed at professionals in professions such as data analysis, computing, engineering and architecture, media and entertainment, finance, artificial intelligence, etc. With a wealth of experience in the sector, we offer personalized support to private and institutional clients. We currently have three stores in Morocco, offering a wide range of premium quality products and services.

    OUR HISTORY

    Game Bit has become a major player in the IT industry in Morocco by specializing in the supply of high-end computer equipment for professionals. Founded in 1998. It opened three specialty stores and launched an online platform in 2023 to provide exceptional service, in-depth expertise and premium products. Game Bit is committed to being the benchmark in high-end computer hardware for professionals in Morocco and contributing to a brighter technological future for its customers.
